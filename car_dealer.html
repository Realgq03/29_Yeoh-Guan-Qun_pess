About Page

Website Information

Simulation and management of car sales in a store

Customer arrives at the store, the customer then proceeds to queue up in the queue

The first customer of the queue will then move to the car they want to purchase

The rest of the customers in the queue will then move up to allow more customers to queue up

The customer at a car will then be asked a series of multiple choice questions

If the customer passed, they will have a choice to purchases the car, else they will have to leave the store.

If customer chose to buy it, they will move to the cashier to pay for it

They will then proceed and exit the store.



Game Explanation


Once the start game button is clicked. You will be directed to the application scene,

Once the application scene is up, a queue will be on the right side of the screen.

You are to drag the client to their desired car brand, once client dropped on their desired car brand.

A question promp will pop up about the car with a series of 5 questions, based on the results.

If the client passed the short quiz, a promp will pop up

The client will then have a choice whether to buy the car or not.

If No is clicked, client goes straight into the exit. Else, client moves to the cashier.

Once client is dropped on the cashier, a promp will pop up asking if client want to purchase the car

If yes is clicked, client will exit the store and the money earned and customer served will update. Else, Client jsut exit the store.
